Literal expressions
Meta-characters: ^, $, |, ()
Need to escape meta-characters to be literals.
Character sets: [ ]
  • Complement of character set: start with ^.
  • Ranges: 0-9, a-f, A-Z
  • Named character sets.
Alternatives: expr1|expr2
  • Use parentheses to group alternatives
  • Can refer to group later in regular expression: \\1, \\2, ..., \\9
Match any character meta-character: .
Quantifiers:
apply to previous sub-expression.
*0 or more occurrences
?0 or 1 occurrence
+one or more, i.e. at least one.
{m}exactly m copies
{m,}at least m (general version of +)
{m,n}at least m, and no more than n, where n >= m
Substitution:
  • Use gsub and sub.
